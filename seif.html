Drop Shipping Explained

The innovative way to run an online business without holding inventory

What is Drop Shipping?

Drop shipping is an advanced business model that allows you to sell products online without maintaining inventory. In this model, you act as an intermediary between the supplier and the customer—the supplier handles packaging and shipping directly, reducing overhead costs and risks while you focus on marketing and customer service.
